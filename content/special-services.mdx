## Usage Tips

<Note>
  Type any service keyword into Nozmie, and it will automatically show you all available options you currently have access to, sorted by cooldown and usefulness.
</Note>

**Common keywords to try:**
- `repair` - Shows all repair options
- `mail` or `mailbox` - Shows all mailbox options
- `transmog` or `mog` - Shows transmog services
- `bank` - Shows bank access options
- `vendor` - Shows vendor access
- `auction` or `ah` - Shows auction house access

<Info>
  Engineering characters have the most service options available. Consider learning Engineering if you want maximum convenience!
</Info>
