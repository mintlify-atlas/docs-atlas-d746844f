## Hearthstones

Nozmie recognizes all hearthstone variants and themed hearthstones.

<Info>
  All hearthstone variants return you to your set inn location. Most have a 30-minute cooldown, while some cosmetic variants have no cooldown.
</Info>

### Standard Hearthstones
- **Hearthstone** - The classic inn keeper item (30 min CD)
- **The Innkeeper's Daughter** - Toy variant (30 min CD)
- **Eternal Traveler's Hearthstone** - Premium variant (30 min CD)

### Seasonal & Event Hearthstones
- **Brewfest Reveler's Hearthstone** - Beer-themed animation
- **Headless Horseman's Hearthstone** - Halloween themed
- **Lunar Elder's Hearthstone** - Lunar New Year themed
- **Peddlefeet's Lovely Hearthstone** - Love is in the Air themed
- **Noble Gardener's Hearthstone** - Noblegarden themed
- **Fire Eater's Hearthstone** - Midsummer Fire Festival themed
- **Greatfather Winter's Hearthstone** - Winter Veil themed

### Special Hearthstones
- **Holographic Digitalization Hearthstone** - Holographic effect
- **Dominated Hearthstone** - Shadowlands themed
- **Enlightened Hearthstone** - Zereth Mortis themed
- **Broker Translocation Matrix** - Broker portal effect
- **Deepdweller's Earthen Hearthstone** - Earthen themed
- **Hearthstone of the Flame** - Flame themed
- **Stone of the Hearth** - Stone animation
- **Timewalker's Hearthstone** - Time travel effect
- **Ohn'ir Windsage's Hearthstone** - Wind effect
- **Notorious Thread's Hearthstone** - Nerubian themed
- **Explosive Hearthstone** - Explosive exit
- **Cosmic Hearthstone** - Space themed
- **Timerunner's Hearthstone** - MoP Remix themed

### No Cooldown Variants
<Note>
  These hearthstone variants share the same function but have cosmetic differences and no cooldown.
</Note>

- **Dark Portal** - Dark portal animation
- **Ethereal Portal** - Ethereal effect
- **Astonishingly Scarlet Slippers** - Ruby slippers click animation
- **Scroll of Town Portal** - Diablo 2 reference
- **Tome of Town Portal** - Book variant
- **Draenic Hologem** - Draenei hologram
- **Path of the Naaru** - Naaru blessing animation
- **Redeployment Module** - Gnomish engineering theme
- **Naaru's Enfold** - Light embrace animation

<Info>
  Try keywords like "hearth", "home", or "inn" to quickly access any hearthstone variant.
</Info>
