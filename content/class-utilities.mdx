Nozmie supports quick access to essential class utilities and buffs. Simply type what you need, and Nozmie will cast the appropriate spell.

## Mage Utilities

Mages provide essential raid buffs, food, and utility spells.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Conjure Refreshment" icon="cookie">

    **Spell ID:** 190336
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Arcane Intellect" icon="brain">
    Provides intellect buff to party/raid members. Keywords: `intellect`, `arcane intellect`, `buff`, `int`
    
    **Spell ID:** 1459
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Slow Fall" icon="feather">

  Mage teleports and portals are covered in the [Teleportation](/content/teleportation) section.
</Info>

## Mass Resurrection

After a wipe, mass resurrection spells can bring the entire raid back quickly.

<Info>
  Mass resurrection spells are priority 1 commands and will be suggested first when typing resurrection keywords.
</Info>

- **Mass Resurrection** (Priest) - Spell ID: 212036
- **Revitalize** (Druid) - Spell ID: 212040
- **Ancestral Vision** (Shaman) - Spell ID: 212048
- **Absolution** (Paladin) - Spell ID: 212056
- **Reawaken** (Monk) - Spell ID: 212051
- **Mass Return** (Evoker) - Spell ID: 361178

Keywords: `mass res`, `rez`, `mass rez`, `mass revive`, `mass ress`
