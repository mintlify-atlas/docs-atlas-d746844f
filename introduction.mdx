## What is Nozmie?

Nozmie is an intelligent World of Warcraft addon that automatically detects teleport, portal, and utility requests from chat messages and displays an interactive banner for instant one-click casting or item usage. It eliminates the hassle of manually targeting players and searching through your spellbook or bags.

## Key Features

### Intelligent Detection

- **Whole-word pattern matching** prevents false positives ("portal" won't match "portable")
- **Multi-select chat channels** to monitor via the settings panel
- **Player targeting** automatically targets the player who made the request
- **Persistent blacklist** to exclude messages containing specific words

### Smart Banner System

- **Stacked banners** display multiple options when several matches are found
- **Portal prioritization** optionally prioritize portal spells over regular teleports
- **Cooldown tracking** with visual feedback on spell icons
- **Auto-hide timeout** configurable banner duration
- **Position persistence** banner position is saved between sessions

### Utility Page UI

Access with `/noz utility` to browse all available content:

- **Current Dungeons**: Mythic+ dungeons currently in rotation
- **Legacy Dungeons**: Mythic+ dungeons from previous seasons
- **Teleports**: Raid teleports, Delve access, and teleportation toys
- **Utility**: Utility spells and services (buffs, summons, repairs, etc.)
- **Hearthstone**: All hearthstone items and variants you own

<Info>
  The Utility Page features a search bar for quick filtering and displays real-time cooldown timers on spell icons.
</Info>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Chat Monitoring">
    Nozmie listens to your configured chat channels and analyzes messages for keywords matching known spells and items.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Keyword Matching">
    Uses intelligent whole-word detection to find exact matches and prevent false positives.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Availability Check">
    Verifies that you own the spell, item, or toy before displaying it in the banner or utility page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Banner Display">
    Shows a classic WoW-style dialog banner with the matched option(s) and their icons.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="One-Click Action">
    Click the banner or utility page item to instantly cast the spell or use the item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Nozmie only displays actions you can actually perform - owned spells, items, or toys. This keeps your interface clean and relevant.
</Note>

## Customization Options

### Suppression Filters

Control what appears in your banners:

- **Mounts** - Hide mount-related utilities
- **Class Utilities** - Suppress class-specific buffs and services
- **Teleports & Portals** - Filter out teleportation options
- **Utility Services** - Hide repair, mailbox, anvil, and transmog options

### Instance-Specific Settings

Apply different filters for:
- Open world content
- Dungeons (including Mythic+)
- Raid encounters

<Info>
  Use instance filters to hide unwanted categories in dungeons/raids while keeping them visible in the open world.
</Info>
